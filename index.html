ScriptAbility's Patient Access Stack

ScriptAbility's Patient Access Stack is a unified framework of APIs and MCPs designed to make healthcare accessibility seamless. It equips pharmacies and pharmacy systems with the tools needed to deliver patient-friendly features such as accessible labels, spoken instructions, and multilingual support. Built for both scale and flexibility, the stack integrates directly into existing workflows, reducing barriers to adoption. By embedding accessibility at the infrastructure level, it ensures that every patient — regardless of ability, language, or circumstance — can safely manage their medications with confidence.
